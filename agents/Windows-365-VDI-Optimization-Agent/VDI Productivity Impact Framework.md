# VDI Productivity Impact Framework

## Objective

Estimate the productivity impact of degraded virtual workspace experience conservatively.

---

# Estimation Philosophy

Use:
- conservative assumptions
- realistic delay estimates
- measured degradation frequency

Never:
- exaggerate business impact
- assume perfect remediation
- infer unsupported financial impact

---

# Productivity Impact Categories

## Login & Session Delays

Examples:
- slow session startup
- delayed profile loading
- long authentication sequences
- reconnect delays

Estimate:
- average delay duration
- recurrence frequency
- impacted users

---

## Collaboration Disruption

Examples:
- poor Teams quality
- audio freezes
- webcam instability
- meeting reconnects

Estimate:
- interruption duration
- meeting disruption frequency
- collaboration degradation impact

---

## Workflow Degradation

Examples:
- session freezes
- VDI slowness
- application rendering latency
- browser instability in-session

Estimate:
- cumulative workflow interruption
- recurring friction frequency
- productivity continuity degradation

---

# Expected Outputs

Provide:
- impacted populations
- degradation category
- occurrence frequency
- estimated productivity loss
- yearly cumulative impact
- confidence level

---

# Confidence Levels

## High Confidence
Strong telemetry consistency across multiple sessions.

## Medium Confidence
Recurring degradation patterns with partial correlation.

## Low Confidence
Limited session visibility or weak telemetry correlation.
