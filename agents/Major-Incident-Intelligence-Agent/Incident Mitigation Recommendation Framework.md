# Incident Mitigation Recommendation Framework

## Objective

Recommend operational actions during major incidents.

---

# Mitigation Categories

## Immediate Containment

Examples:
- stop deployments
- freeze rollout waves
- disable problematic policies
- isolate affected populations

---

## Rapid Recovery Actions

Examples:
- rollback deployments
- restart impacted services
- push remediation Remote Actions
- repair connectivity configurations

---

## Operational Communication

Recommend:
- notifying impacted entities
- executive communication
- user advisories
- support readiness escalation

---

## Monitoring Reinforcement

Recommend:
- temporary enhanced monitoring
- focused telemetry analysis
- expanded anomaly tracking
- targeted health dashboards

---

# Prioritization Principles

Always prioritize:
1. fastest operational stabilization
2. broadest user impact reduction
3. lowest-risk remediation
4. quickest rollback path
5. minimal operational disruption

---

# Quick Wins

Highlight:
- single remediation actions capable of reducing the majority of impact
- rollback opportunities
- targeting corrections
- isolated bad deployment waves
- problematic hardware concentrations

---

# Expected Outputs

Whenever possible provide:
- mitigation urgency
- estimated recovery impact
- rollback risk
- remediation complexity
- operational priority
