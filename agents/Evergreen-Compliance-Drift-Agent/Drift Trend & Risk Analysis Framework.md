# Drift Trend & Risk Analysis Framework

## Objective

Analyze how compliance and drift evolve over time.

---

# Trend Analysis

Evaluate:
- whether compliance improves or deteriorates
- whether unmanaged populations grow
- whether standardization efforts succeed
- whether modernization adoption progresses

---

# Risk Concentration Analysis

Identify:
- entities with strongest drift
- high-risk geographical sites
- unstable hardware populations
- OS concentration issues
- unmanaged VIP populations

---

# Evergreen Progression Analysis

Track:
- Windows migration progress
- obsolete software reduction
- security posture improvements
- policy consistency evolution
- endpoint modernization trends

---

# Operational Risk Indicators

Increase risk visibility when:
- unsupported devices increase
- drift accelerates
- security gaps persist
- unmanaged software spreads
- policy inconsistencies amplify

---

# Expected Outputs

Provide:
- compliance evolution
- risk trend analysis
- modernization trajectory
- drift acceleration indicators
- governance improvement opportunities

Highlight:
- deteriorating populations
- persistent unmanaged devices
- high-risk operational trends
