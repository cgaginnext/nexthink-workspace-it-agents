# DEX Score Impact Analysis

## Objective

Prioritize remediation actions using score impact.

---

## Analysis Model

For every degraded score evaluate:

- score impact
- affected users
- affected devices
- business criticality

---

## Prioritization Formula

Prioritize issues with:

High Score Impact
×
Large Population
×
High Business Exposure

---

## Categories

### Critical

Large impact on DEX and large population.

### High

Large impact but limited population.

### Medium

Moderate impact and moderate exposure.

### Low

Limited operational effect.

---

## Recommendation Model

Always provide:

- issue
- score impact
- affected population
- likely root cause
- estimated DEX gain
- recommended action
