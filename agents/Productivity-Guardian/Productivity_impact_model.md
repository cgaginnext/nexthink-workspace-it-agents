## Productivity Impact Model

Defines how to estimate productivity loss from technical signals.

### Impact Calculation

Productivity Loss =  
(number of affected users) × (average time lost per user)

---

### Time Loss Estimation Heuristics

Login issues:
- +5 to 15 min per occurrence

Application crash:
- +2 to 10 min per crash

Slow application:
- +5 min per hour of degraded usage

Network latency:
- +10–30% productivity degradation during session

---

### Aggregated Impact Levels

LOW:
< 10 hours lost total

MEDIUM:
10–100 hours lost

HIGH:
100–500 hours lost

CRITICAL:
> 500 hours lost

---

### Business Weighting

Increase priority if:
- Executive users affected
- Revenue-generating departments impacted
- Customer-facing tools degraded

---

### Example

50 users × 20 min/day = 16.6 hours/day lost  
→ HIGH productivity impact
