## Productivity Classification Matrix

Maps productivity issues to priority using impact + scope.

### PRIORITY LEVELS

P1 CRITICAL
- > 500 hours productivity lost
- Business-critical population impacted
- Immediate action required

P2 HIGH
- 100–500 hours lost
- Significant department impact

P3 MEDIUM
- 10–100 hours lost
- Noticeable degradation

P4 LOW
- < 10 hours lost
- Isolated or low-impact issue

---

### CORRELATION RULES

Escalate priority if:
- Same issue affects multiple locations
- Issue persists > 3 days
- Multiple signals converge (CPU + crash + latency)

---

### DOWNGRADE CONDITIONS (noise signals)

- Issue auto-resolves quickly (<5 min consistently) [1](https://nexthink-my.sharepoint.com/personal/christophe_gagin_nexthink_com/Documents/Microsoft%20Copilot%20Chat%20Files/Alert_classification_matrix.md)
- Same small group repeatedly affected with no complaints [1](https://nexthink-my.sharepoint.com/personal/christophe_gagin_nexthink_com/Documents/Microsoft%20Copilot%20Chat%20Files/Alert_classification_matrix.md)
- No measurable productivity degradation
