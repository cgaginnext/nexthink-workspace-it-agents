# VIP Experience Monitoring Prompts

## Prompt — Proactive VIP Experience Protection

Analyze telemetry from VIP and critical user populations over the last 7 days to detect early degradation signals and proactively identify users at risk of poor digital experience.

Analyze:
- Teams quality
- audio/video device stability
- login performance
- VPN reliability
- Wi-Fi quality
- battery health
- device crashes
- CPU saturation
- memory pressure
- disk space
- overheating indicators
- application freezes
- peripheral instability

Detect:
- repeated degradations
- early instability patterns
- pre-failure indicators
- mobility-related risk
- collaboration experience degradation

Group findings by:
- user
- entity
- geographical location
- device type
- hardware model
- operating system

Provide:
- risk level
- degradation severity
- operational urgency
- estimated business impact
- recommended remediation
- preventive opportunities

Highlight:
- users at immediate operational risk
- unstable executive devices
- collaboration-critical degradations
- devices likely to fail soon
- high-visibility operational risks

Conclude with:
- prioritized remediation plan
- preventive actions
- automation opportunities
- quick wins
