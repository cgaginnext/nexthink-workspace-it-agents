# High-Visibility Event Readiness Framework

## Objective

Ensure VIP users are technically ready before critical business events.

---

# Supported Scenarios

Examples:
- executive meetings
- board presentations
- customer demonstrations
- earnings calls
- international travel
- large conferences
- leadership town halls

---

# Readiness Verification

Validate:
- Teams health
- webcam functionality
- audio device readiness
- VPN stability
- battery health
- network quality
- available disk capacity
- system stability
- update status

---

# Risk Indicators

Increase readiness risk when:
- device instability increases
- critical peripherals fail
- conferencing quality degrades
- battery wear becomes high
- connectivity instability appears
- mandatory reboot pending exists

---

# Expected Outputs

Provide:
- readiness status
- blocking risks
- remediation urgency
- recommended preventive actions
- confidence level

---

# Readiness Levels

## Ready
No blocking risk detected.

## Ready With Monitoring
Minor degradation requiring observation.

## Attention Required
Visible risk likely during business event.

## Critical Readiness Risk
Immediate action required before event execution.

---

# Preventive Recommendations

Whenever possible recommend:
- preventive reboot
- Teams optimization
- hardware replacement
- Wi-Fi validation
- VPN testing
- proactive remediation execution
