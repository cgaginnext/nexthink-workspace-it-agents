Recommend preventive remediation actions for VIP populations before incidents become visible.

- browser cleanup

# Automation Recommendations

Whenever possible suggest:
- proactive Remote Actions
- automated health remediation
- preventive campaigns
- executive readiness checks
- scheduled optimization workflows

---

# Prioritization Principles

Always prioritize:
1. visible experience protection
2. prevention before disruption
3. lowest operational effort
4. highest executive exposure
5. fastest user stabilization

Highlight:
- quick wins
- proactive fixes
- low-risk automations
- high-visibility prevention opportunities
