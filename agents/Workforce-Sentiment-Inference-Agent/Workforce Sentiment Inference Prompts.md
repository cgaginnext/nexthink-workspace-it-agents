# Workforce Sentiment Inference Prompts

## Global Workforce Friction & Fatigue Assessment

Analyze endpoint telemetry and employee behavioral patterns over the last 90 days to identify populations exposed to recurring digital friction and operational fatigue.

Analyze:
- repeated crashes
- excessive login interruptions
- VPN reconnect frequency
- repeated Teams degradation
- poor collaboration sessions
- workflow abandonment
- repetitive failed transactions
- excessive authentication prompts
- repeated reboot requirements
- recurring self-healing events
- application instability
- unresolved recurring degradations

Measure:
- interruption frequency
- cumulative friction exposure
- recurring degradation duration
- silent recovery behaviors
- support request absence despite visible friction

Identify:
- silent strugglers
- high-friction populations
- operational overload clusters
- technology fatigue patterns
- employees repeatedly impacted without escalation

Group findings by:
- workplace persona
- business entity
- geographical location
- collaboration intensity
- device type
- operating system

Provide:
- operational friction severity
- cumulative interruption indicators
- employee exposure trends
- likely digital fatigue contributors
- remediation confidence level

Highlight:
- populations heavily exposed to recurring friction
- users repeatedly impacted by collaboration issues
- high interruption environments
- departments suffering persistent digital degradation
- underreported operational pain

Conclude with:
- proactive support recommendations
- Engage outreach opportunities
- workflow simplification opportunities
- friction reduction priorities
