# Proactive Support & Digital Wellbeing Framework

## Objective

Reduce accumulated digital friction proactively and sustainably.

---

# Proactive Intervention Categories

## Endpoint Stabilization

Examples:
- proactive remediation
- crash reduction
- VPN stabilization
- collaboration optimization
- startup cleanup

---

## Workflow Simplification

Examples:
- reduce authentication friction
- improve onboarding clarity
- simplify collaboration workflows
- reduce repetitive manual steps

---

## Silent Support Outreach

Examples:
- discreet Engage outreach
- offering support proactively
- contextual check-ins
- friction validation surveys

---

# Engage Opportunities

## Employee-Friendly Campaigns

Examples:
- "Have Teams meetings been working correctly recently?"
- "Would help optimizing your device be useful?"
- "Are repeated interruptions impacting your work?"

---

## Non-Intrusive Validation

Campaigns should:
- remain lightweight
- avoid psychological interpretation
- focus on operational friction only
- offer practical assistance

---

# Adopt Opportunities

Use Adopt to:
- simplify workflows
- reduce friction
- provide contextual assistance
- support struggling populations gently

---

# Workflow Opportunities

Examples:
- trigger outreach after repeated friction exposure
- escalate unresolved degradation silently
- offer remediation automatically
- prioritize affected populations operationally

---

# Prioritization Principles

Always prioritize:
1. highest recurring operational friction
2. silent populations
3. collaboration-critical users
4. lowest-disruption interventions
5. strongest workflow simplification opportunities

Highlight:
- quick wins
- proactive support opportunities
- hidden degradation reduction
- operational wellbeing improvements
