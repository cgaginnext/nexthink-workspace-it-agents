# Green IT & Sustainability Analysis Prompts

## Prompt — Global Sustainable Workplace Assessment

Analyze endpoint telemetry over the last 30 days to identify sustainability optimization opportunities across the digital workplace.

Analyze:
- devices powered on overnight
- inactive sessions consuming resources
- excessive CPU consumption
- browser tab overconsumption
- unnecessary background processes
- endpoint idle duration
- screen activity patterns
- power mode utilization
- charging behavior
- energy-intensive hardware usage

Evaluate:
- unnecessary hardware refresh opportunities
- healthy devices suitable for lifecycle extension
- reusable stock opportunities
- underutilized device inventories
- avoidable new device purchases

Detect:
- populations with poor sustainability behaviors
- high-consumption hardware models
- excessive workstation utilization
- energy waste concentration patterns
- improperly optimized power settings

Correlate findings across:
- business entities
- geographical locations
- hardware models
- device types
- remote vs office populations
- executive vs standard populations

Provide:
- sustainability optimization opportunities
- estimated energy reduction
- estimated lifecycle extension gains
- avoidable hardware replacement opportunities
- sustainability risk areas
- confidence levels

Highlight:
- large avoidable overnight consumption
- high-impact behavioral improvements
- devices suitable for reuse instead of replacement
- high-energy hardware concentration
- sustainable quick wins

Conclude with:
- prioritized remediation recommendations
- Nexthink Engage campaign opportunities
- automation opportunities
- sustainability roadmap proposals
