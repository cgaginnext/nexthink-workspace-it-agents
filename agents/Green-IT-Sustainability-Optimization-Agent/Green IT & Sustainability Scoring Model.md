# Green IT & Sustainability Scoring Model

## Objective

Measure endpoint sustainability posture and identify optimization opportunities.

---

# Sustainability Signals

## Energy Consumption Signals

Decrease sustainability score when:
- devices remain powered on overnight
- idle sessions consume resources excessively
- high-performance modes remain permanently enabled
- peripherals remain active unnecessarily
- CPU usage stays abnormally elevated during inactivity

---

## Behavioral Optimization Signals

Decrease score when:
- browser tab overconsumption becomes excessive
- unnecessary collaboration sessions remain active
- startup applications overload systems
- users never reboot devices
- resource-heavy applications remain inactive but loaded

---

## Hardware Lifecycle Signals

Decrease score when:
- healthy devices are replaced prematurely
- stock reuse opportunities are ignored
- devices with healthy DEX scores are refreshed unnecessarily
- lifecycle extension possibilities are not leveraged

---

## Sustainable Governance Signals

Decrease score when:
- power policies are inconsistent
- sustainability campaigns are absent
- unmanaged populations consume excessive resources
- eco-friendly configurations are underused

---

# Sustainability Dimensions

## Energy Efficiency
How optimized endpoint energy usage is.

## Behavioral Sustainability
How responsibly digital tools are used.

## Lifecycle Sustainability
How effectively hardware lifespan is maximized.

## Governance Maturity
How well sustainable workplace practices are enforced.

---

# Sustainability Levels

## Highly Optimized
Strong sustainable workplace posture.

## Moderately Optimized
Some avoidable waste remains.

## Improvement Needed
Meaningful sustainability opportunities exist.

## Poor Sustainability Posture
Large avoidable environmental impact detected.

---

# Prioritization Logic

Prioritize:
1. highest cumulative environmental impact
2. avoidable hardware refresh
3. large user populations
4. easiest implementation effort
5. strongest behavioral improvement opportunity
