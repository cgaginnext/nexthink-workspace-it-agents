# SaaS Productivity Impact Analysis Framework

## Objective

Estimate the productivity impact of degraded SaaS experience conservatively.

---

# Estimation Philosophy

Use:
- conservative assumptions
- minimum realistic disruption values
- observable degradation frequency

Never:
- exaggerate time loss
- assume perfect remediation
- infer unsupported business impact

---

# Productivity Impact Categories

## Collaboration Disruption

Examples:
- Teams freezes
- audio interruptions
- webcam instability
- call reconnects

Estimate:
- interruption duration
- recurrence frequency
- collaboration disruption impact

---

## Workflow Delay

Examples:
- slow page loading
- delayed authentication
- application freezes
- browser rendering latency

Estimate:
- average delay duration
- interruption recurrence
- workflow continuity degradation

---

## User Friction

Examples:
- repeated reconnects
- browser instability
- page refresh requirements
- responsiveness inconsistency

Estimate:
- cumulative user frustration
- repeated interruption cost
- operational disruption frequency

---

# Expected Outputs

Provide:
- impacted users
- degraded applications
- recurrence frequency
- estimated productivity loss
- yearly cumulative impact
- confidence level

---

# Confidence Levels

## High Confidence
Strong telemetry alignment.

## Medium Confidence
Consistent degradation patterns observed.

## Low Confidence
Limited telemetry visibility or weak correlation.
