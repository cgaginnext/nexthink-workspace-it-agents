# AI Adoption & Alternative Strategy Framework

## Objective

Align employee AI adoption with secure, governed, and productive enterprise workflows.

---

# Core Philosophy

Employees adopting external AI tools often indicate:
- unmet productivity needs
- missing automation
- poor workflow efficiency
- insufficient internal enablement

The objective is not only to restrict usage, but also to:
- understand why adoption happens
- address productivity demand
- improve approved alternatives

---

# AI Adoption Areas

## Enterprise AI Alignment

Evaluate:
- Copilot adoption
- external AI dependency
- duplicate AI assistant usage
- unmanaged AI workflows

---

## Productivity Opportunity Analysis

Identify:
- repetitive workflows suitable for AI assistance
- departments strongly adopting AI
- populations with high AI productivity demand
- workflows poorly supported internally

---

## Alternative Migration Opportunities

Recommend:
- Copilot onboarding
- Microsoft 365 AI enablement
- approved workflow redesign
- secure AI alternatives

---

# Expected Outputs

Provide:
- external AI adoption levels
- internal AI adoption maturity
- migration opportunities
- enablement priorities
- confidence levels

---

# Confidence Levels

## High Confidence
Strong recurring telemetry and usage consistency.

## Medium Confidence
Partial workflow visibility.

## Low Confidence
Limited telemetry or fragmented visibility.
