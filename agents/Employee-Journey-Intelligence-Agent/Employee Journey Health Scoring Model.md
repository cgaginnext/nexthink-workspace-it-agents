# Employee Journey Health Scoring Model

## Objective

Measure the quality and success of employee transitions and workplace transformations.

---

# Journey Health Signals

## Productivity Recovery Signals

Increase journey score when:
- application readiness is fast
- workflow continuity remains stable
- collaboration quality is preserved
- employees stabilize rapidly

Decrease score when:
- crashes increase
- workflows fail repeatedly
- login friction appears
- application instability persists

---

## Adoption Signals

Increase adoption score when:
- new tools are actively used
- workflows modernize successfully
- repeated usage grows
- support dependency decreases

Decrease score when:
- users revert to old workflows
- applications are abandoned
- remediation recurrence remains high

---

## Collaboration Readiness Signals

Increase readiness score when:
- Teams quality remains healthy
- peripherals function correctly
- VPN stability remains strong
- conferencing workflows remain smooth

---

## Stability Signals

Decrease journey stability when:
- post-migration crashes amplify
- authentication loops appear
- background remediation persists
- repeated support interventions occur

---

# Journey Dimensions

## Readiness
How prepared the employee/device was before transition.

## Transition Quality
How smooth execution remained.

## Stabilization Speed
How quickly productivity recovered.

## Long-Term Adoption
Whether the change remained successful over time.

---

# Journey Levels

## Excellent Journey
Very low friction and rapid adaptation.

## Acceptable Journey
Minor adaptation challenges only.

## Fragile Journey
Recurring instability and friction.

## Failed Journey
Persistent operational degradation or adoption failure.

---

# Prioritization Logic

Prioritize:
1. failed journeys
2. high-visibility populations
3. recurring stabilization failures
4. large impacted populations
5. easiest recovery opportunities
