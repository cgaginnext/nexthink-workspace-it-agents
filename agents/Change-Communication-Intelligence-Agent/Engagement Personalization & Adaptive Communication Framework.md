# Engagement Personalization & Adaptive Communication Framework

## Objective

Improve change adoption through contextual, persona-aware, and behavior-sensitive communication.

---

# Personalization Strategy

## Persona-Aware Communication

Adapt campaigns according to:
- collaboration-heavy users
- highly mobile workers
- low-tech users
- executives
- remote populations
- VDI-intensive employees

---

## Behavioral Awareness

Adjust communication using:
- historical response patterns
- campaign engagement history
- digital friction sensitivity
- adoption maturity
- support dependency

---

## Progressive Communication Journeys

Structure campaigns in phases:
1. awareness
2. preparation
3. guidance
4. transition
5. reinforcement

Avoid:
- isolated one-shot communication
- repetitive spam-like campaigns

---

# Engage Optimization Opportunities

## Adaptive Targeting

Examples:
- suppress campaigns for highly overloaded populations
- prioritize silent strugglers
- personalize communication intensity
- trigger contextual reminders only when needed

---

## Smart Surveys

Examples:
- migration sentiment validation
- transformation confidence measurement
- workflow pain collection
- readiness assessments

---

# Adopt Opportunities

## Contextual Guidance

Use Adopt to:
- reduce communication overload
- provide in-app assistance
- educate at the moment of need
- reinforce behavioral transformation

---

## Guided Adoption Journeys

Examples:
- Copilot onboarding
- Teams collaboration guidance
- hybrid work adaptation
- migration assistance

---

# Automation Opportunities

Examples:
- trigger Engage after failed adoption behavior
- escalate repeated communication avoidance
- launch contextual guidance dynamically
- personalize reinforcement automatically

---

# Prioritization Principles

Always prioritize:
1. highest behavioral impact
2. lowest communication noise
3. strongest personalization opportunities
4. lowest employee disruption
5. measurable adoption outcomes

Highlight:
- quick wins
- communication simplification opportunities
- adaptive engagement workflows
- fatigue reduction strategies
