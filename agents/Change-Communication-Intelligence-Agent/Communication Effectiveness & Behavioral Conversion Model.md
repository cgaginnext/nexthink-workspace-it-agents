# Communication Effectiveness & Behavioral Conversion Model

## Objective

Measure whether workplace communication generates meaningful employee action and behavioral change.

---

# Communication Signals

## Engagement Signals

Increase effectiveness score when:
- campaigns are opened rapidly
- employees complete actions quickly
- response rates remain high
- repeated engagement persists

Decrease score when:
- campaigns are ignored
- dismiss rates increase
- engagement drops over time
- follow-up interaction disappears

---

## Behavioral Conversion Signals

Increase communication value when:
- targeted behaviors improve
- workflows modernize
- adoption stabilizes
- remediation completion increases

Decrease value when:
- old behaviors persist
- temporary change disappears
- resistance increases
- support dependency remains unchanged

---

## Fatigue Signals

Increase fatigue risk when:
- campaign density becomes excessive
- interaction delays increase
- engagement quality decreases
- populations systematically ignore messages

---

## Timing Signals

Increase communication efficiency when:
- messages align with employee availability
- campaigns avoid meeting-heavy windows
- populations interact naturally without reminders

---

# Effectiveness Dimensions

## Awareness
Did employees see the communication?

## Engagement
Did they interact meaningfully?

## Conversion
Did behavior actually change?

## Durability
Did behavioral improvement persist?

## Fatigue Risk
Is communication volume degrading effectiveness?

---

# Communication Maturity Levels

## High-Impact Communication
Strong durable behavioral outcomes.

## Moderate Effectiveness
Partial engagement and inconsistent behavioral adoption.

## Low Effectiveness
Weak operational impact.

## Counterproductive Communication
Communication generating disengagement or resistance.

---

# Prioritization Logic

Prioritize:
1. highest behavioral impact opportunities
2. over-communicated populations
3. transformation-critical campaigns
4. weakest conversion areas
5. easiest personalization opportunities
