# Time Savings Estimation Framework

## Objective

Estimate operational and employee productivity gains conservatively.

---

# IT Time Savings

Estimate:
- avoided ticket handling time
- avoided troubleshooting
- avoided escalation effort
- reduced manual remediation effort

Use:
- minimum realistic handling times
- conservative assumptions

---

# Employee Productivity Savings

Estimate:
- reduced interruption time
- reduced downtime
- faster recovery time
- avoided recurring friction

Use:
- minimum productivity assumptions
- cautious interruption estimates

---

# Estimation Principles

Never:
- use optimistic assumptions
- extrapolate unsupported gains
- assume full automation adoption

Always:
- explain estimation methodology
- provide unit gain assumptions
- distinguish IT vs employee gains

---

# Output Expectations

Provide:
- incident type
- estimated incidents avoided
- IT minutes saved per incident
- employee minutes saved per incident
- total IT hours saved
- total employee hours recovered
- confidence level

---

# Confidence Levels

## High Confidence
Historical remediation success already demonstrated.

## Medium Confidence
Repeatability strongly observed but partial unknowns remain.

## Low Confidence
Limited telemetry or inconsistent remediation success.
