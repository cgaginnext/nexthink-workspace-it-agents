# Workplace Optimization Strategy Framework

## Objective

Optimize workplace costs while preserving employee productivity and digital experience quality.

---

# Optimization Categories

## Hardware Optimization

Examples:
- endpoint right-sizing
- stock reutilization
- hardware standardization
- lifecycle extension
- underutilized asset recovery

---

## Software & License Optimization

Examples:
- license reclamation
- duplicate SaaS reduction
- platform consolidation
- premium license reassignment
- low-value subscription cleanup

---

## Operational Efficiency Optimization

Examples:
- self-healing expansion
- unstable hardware reduction
- proactive remediation
- automation adoption
- recurring support reduction

---

## Infrastructure Optimization

Examples:
- VDI resource resizing
- cloud PC optimization
- workload balancing
- idle resource cleanup
- endpoint performance tuning

---

## Sustainability Optimization

Examples:
- energy reduction
- overnight shutdown optimization
- hardware lifecycle extension
- reuse-first procurement

---

# Employee Protection Principles

Always:
- protect critical workflows
- preserve collaboration quality
- avoid aggressive optimization
- validate productivity impact

Optimization must:
- remain minimally disruptive
- prioritize user experience stability
- maintain operational continuity

---

# Automation Opportunities

Whenever possible recommend:
- Remote Actions
- reclamation workflows
- automated cleanup
- adaptive scaling
- lifecycle automation
- proactive remediation

---

# Prioritization Principles

Always prioritize:
1. highest waste reduction
2. lowest employee impact
3. easiest implementation
4. broadest optimization scope
5. strongest ROI

Highlight:
- quick wins
- low-risk optimizations
- automation candidates
- governance improvements
