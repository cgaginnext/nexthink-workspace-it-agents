# Workplace Persona Intelligence Prompts

## Global Persona Discovery

Analyze endpoint telemetry and behavioral patterns over the last 90 days to identify operational workplace personas across the organization.

Analyze:
- collaboration intensity
- mobility behavior
- VPN usage
- application usage patterns
- remote work frequency
- VDI dependency
- hardware utilization
- endpoint stability
- digital friction exposure
- support dependency

Identify:
- highly mobile employees
- collaboration-heavy populations
- fragile IT users
- VDI-intensive workers
- low-tech users
- developer-like behaviors
- shift-based populations
- office-centric employees

Group findings by:
- business entity
- geographical site
- device type
- OS version
- hardware model
- collaboration patterns

Provide:
- detected personas
- behavioral characteristics
- population size
- operational sensitivities
- optimization opportunities
- confidence level

Highlight:
- high-support personas
- high-friction workstyles
- collaboration-critical populations
- operationally sensitive groups
- highly productive technology patterns

Conclude with:
- targeting recommendations
- Engage opportunities
- Adopt opportunities
- operational segmentation strategies
