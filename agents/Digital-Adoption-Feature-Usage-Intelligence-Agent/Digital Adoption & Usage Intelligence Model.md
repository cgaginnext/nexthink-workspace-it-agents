# Digital Adoption & Usage Intelligence Model

## Objective

Measure technology adoption maturity and identify unrealized workplace value.

---

# Adoption Signals

## Feature Utilization

Increase adoption score when:
- advanced features are actively used
- collaboration behaviors evolve positively
- workflows modernize
- usage depth increases

Decrease score when:
- only basic usage exists
- advanced capabilities remain unused
- legacy workflows persist
- high-value features are ignored

---

## Behavioral Modernization

Increase maturity when:
- cloud collaboration replaces attachments
- Teams channels replace fragmented communication
- collaborative editing becomes common
- Copilot usage becomes contextual and recurrent

---

## Productivity Efficiency

Increase efficiency score when:
- workflows simplify
- transaction completion improves
- collaboration becomes faster
- cross-app usage becomes fluid

Decrease score when:
- repetitive manual behaviors persist
- users duplicate workflows unnecessarily
- applications are underused despite licensing

---

## Adoption Friction Signals

Decrease adoption confidence when:
- application crashes increase
- workflows are abandoned
- authentication friction appears
- features are tried but abandoned
- transaction times degrade

---

# Adoption Dimensions

## Activation
Whether users tried the capability.

## Recurrence
Whether usage becomes habitual.

## Depth
How advanced and diversified usage becomes.

## Business Integration
How embedded the capability becomes in workflows.

## Resistance
Whether users revert to previous behaviors.

---

# Adoption Levels

## Mature Adoption
Strong recurring and diversified usage.

## Emerging Adoption
Capabilities increasingly integrated.

## Weak Adoption
Limited or superficial usage.

## Failed Adoption
Capabilities deployed but ignored or abandoned.

---

# Prioritization Logic

Prioritize:
1. expensive unused licenses
2. high-value feature gaps
3. large populations
4. recurring inefficient workflows
5. easiest adoption acceleration opportunities
