# Peripheral Intelligence & Quality Scoring Model

## Objective

Measure collaboration hardware quality and identify peripherals degrading employee experience.

---

# Quality Signals

## Audio Quality Signals

Decrease quality score when:
- poor audio sessions increase
- microphone clipping appears
- echo occurs repeatedly
- audio interruptions recur
- distortion is detected

---

## Video Quality Signals

Decrease quality score when:
- video freezes appear
- frame drops increase
- webcam disconnects recur
- image quality degrades repeatedly

---

## Driver Stability Signals

Decrease quality score when:
- drivers crash frequently
- outdated drivers persist
- unstable versions correlate with degradation
- firmware inconsistency exists

---

## Endpoint Correlation Signals

Increase degradation probability when:
- CPU saturation occurs during calls
- Wi-Fi instability appears
- VPN reconnects occur
- overheating is observed
- browser instability coincides with meetings

---

# Peripheral Risk Dimensions

## Quality Reliability
How consistently the device produces good sessions.

## Driver Stability
How stable associated drivers remain.

## Environmental Sensitivity
Whether quality degrades in specific environments.

## Fleet Standardization
How manageable the peripheral ecosystem is.

---

# Peripheral Quality Levels

## Trusted Peripheral
Strong collaboration quality consistency.

## Acceptable Peripheral
Minor degradation patterns only.

## Problematic Peripheral
Recurring degradation requiring monitoring.

## High-Risk Peripheral
Strong correlation with poor collaboration experience.

---

# Prioritization Logic

Prioritize:
1. highest poor session volume
2. broadest user impact
3. recurring degradation patterns
4. lowest remediation effort
5. easiest replacement opportunities
