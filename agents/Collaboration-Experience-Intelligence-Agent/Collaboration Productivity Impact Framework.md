# Collaboration Productivity Impact Framework

## Objective

Estimate productivity loss generated by poor collaboration experience conservatively.

---

# Estimation Philosophy

Use:
- conservative assumptions
- measured degradation frequency
- minimum realistic interruption values

Never:
- exaggerate meeting impact
- assume full meeting failure
- infer unsupported business impact

---

# Productivity Impact Categories

## Meeting Disruption

Examples:
- audio interruptions
- reconnects
- webcam failures
- frozen calls

Estimate:
- interruption duration
- recovery time
- participant disruption

---

## Collaboration Friction

Examples:
- repeated poor sessions
- degraded communication quality
- repeated peripheral switching
- unnecessary troubleshooting during calls

Estimate:
- cumulative meeting friction
- recurring user frustration
- workflow disruption

---

## Hidden Productivity Loss

Examples:
- users tolerating bad peripherals
- recurring unreported degradation
- repeated low-quality meetings

Estimate:
- silent productivity erosion
- cumulative employee interruption
- collaboration fatigue

---

# Expected Outputs

Provide:
- impacted populations
- total duration of poor sessions
- recurrence frequency
- estimated productivity loss
- yearly cumulative impact
- confidence level

---

# Confidence Levels

## High Confidence
Strong telemetry consistency and repeated degradation patterns.

## Medium Confidence
Recurring symptoms with partial telemetry evidence.

## Low Confidence
Limited visibility or low degradation recurrence.
