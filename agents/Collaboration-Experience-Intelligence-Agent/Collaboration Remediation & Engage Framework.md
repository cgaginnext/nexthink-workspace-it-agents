# Collaboration Remediation & Engage Framework

## Objective

Recommend operational improvements and proactive engagement actions for collaboration experience optimization.

---

# Remediation Categories

## Peripheral Replacement

Examples:
- replace unstable headsets
- retire problematic webcams
- standardize high-quality peripherals
- reduce unsupported hardware diversity

---

## Driver Remediation

Examples:
- audio driver updates
- camera firmware alignment
- rollback unstable driver versions
- standardize validated driver baselines

---

## Endpoint Optimization

Examples:
- CPU optimization
- browser cleanup
- memory remediation
- thermal management
- startup optimization

---

## Connectivity Optimization

Examples:
- Wi-Fi remediation
- VPN stabilization
- DNS optimization
- conferencing QoS improvements

---

# Nexthink Engage Opportunities

## Awareness Campaigns

Examples:
- headset replacement recommendations
- collaboration best practices
- docking station reminders
- Wi-Fi optimization education

---

## Proactive Outreach Campaigns

Examples:
- contact silent sufferers
- validate employee sentiment after poor sessions
- confirm replacement success
- collect user feedback after remediation

---

# Automation Recommendations

Whenever possible recommend:
- automated driver deployments
- proactive peripheral health checks
- self-healing workflows
- collaboration readiness checks
- scheduled endpoint optimization

---

# Prioritization Principles

Always prioritize:
1. highest collaboration impact
2. lowest employee disruption
3. easiest remediation
4. broadest population impact
5. highest meeting productivity gain

Highlight:
- quick wins
- standardization opportunities
- automation candidates
- proactive outreach opportunities
