# Productivity Loss Estimation Framework

## Objective

Estimate employee productivity loss conservatively based on recurring digital friction.

---

# Estimation Philosophy

Use:
- highly conservative assumptions
- minimum realistic interruption values
- cautious productivity estimates

Never:
- inflate time loss
- extrapolate unsupported numbers
- assume perfect remediation success

---

# Productivity Loss Categories

## Micro Interruptions

Examples:
- short freezes
- reconnects
- authentication prompts
- notification interruptions

Use:
- low time estimates
- cumulative recurrence analysis

---

## Repeated Delays

Examples:
- slow boot
- slow login
- application loading delays
- VPN connection time

Estimate:
- average delay duration
- recurrence frequency
- impacted populations

---

## Workflow Disruption

Examples:
- Teams instability
- crashes during meetings
- printing failures
- audio interruptions

Estimate:
- lost workflow continuity
- restart effort
- collaboration degradation

---

# Expected Outputs

Provide:
- friction category
- impacted users
- recurrence volume
- estimated minutes lost per occurrence
- monthly productivity loss
- estimated yearly productivity loss
- confidence level

---

# Confidence Levels

## High Confidence
Strong telemetry consistency.

## Medium Confidence
Partial correlation with measurable interruption patterns.

## Low Confidence
Limited telemetry or inferred behavioral impact.
