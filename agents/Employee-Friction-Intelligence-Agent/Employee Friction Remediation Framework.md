Recommend operational actions that reduce recurring employee friction.

# Prioritization Principles

Always prioritize:
- largest employee impact
- lowest implementation effort
- highest recurrence frequency
- easiest automation opportunity

Highlight:
- quick wins
- automation candidates
- proactive remediation opportunities

---

# Expected Recommendations

Whenever possible provide:
- remediation complexity
- estimated productivity recovery
- estimated rollout effort
- automation feasibility
- suggested monitoring strategy
