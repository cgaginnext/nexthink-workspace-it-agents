# Nexthink AI Workplace Intelligence Agents

## 1. Productivity Guardian
Protects overall employee productivity by identifying business risks caused by digital friction, collaboration degradation, interruptions, and inefficient workflows. Acts as an executive-level productivity risk intelligence layer.

---

## 2. Endpoint Security Posture Auditor
Evaluates endpoint security posture through compliance, agent health, configuration consistency, operational hygiene, and user behaviors. Bridges the gap between EUC operations and SecOps visibility.

---

## 3. Alert Fatigue Manager
Analyzes operational noise, ignored alerts, and low-value monitoring events to reduce alert fatigue for IT teams. Helps prioritize actionable signals and improve operational focus.

---

## 4. Live Dashboard Builder
Dynamically creates Nexthink dashboards from natural language requests. Enables rapid generation of highly contextualized operational and executive reporting views.

---

## 5. Value Realization Agent
Measures the real business value delivered by Nexthink through DEX improvements, ticket reduction, automation gains, productivity improvements, and adoption metrics. Ideal for QBRs, renewals, and ROI reporting.

---

## 6. Remote Actions Intelligence Agent
Analyzes the quality, effectiveness, usage, and gaps of Nexthink Remote Actions. Identifies optimization opportunities, automation gaps, script reliability, and operational standardization opportunities.

---

## 7. Change Risk & Deployment Readiness Agent
Assesses deployment risks before Windows, security, or application rollouts by identifying fragile devices and sensitive populations. Recommends exclusions, remediation actions, and intelligent rollout strategies.

---

## 8. Ticket Deflection & Self-Healing Opportunity Agent
Identifies repetitive incidents and the best automation or self-healing candidates. Prioritizes remediation opportunities based on ROI, support reduction, and employee experience improvement.

---

## 9. Employee Friction Intelligence Agent
Detects hidden day-to-day digital friction such as slowness, freezes, unstable Wi-Fi, Teams interruptions, repeated authentication prompts, and recurring degradations that silently impact productivity.

---

## 10. Major Incident Intelligence Agent
Correlates weak operational signals to rapidly detect major incidents. Estimates blast radius, impacted populations, degradation severity, and probable root cause hypotheses.

---

## 11. Evergreen Compliance & Drift Agent
Monitors deviations between enterprise standards and real fleet configurations across OS versions, applications, security posture, Intune/GPO settings, and operational consistency.

---

## 12. VIP Experience Protection Agent
Proactively protects executives and business-critical users by detecting degradation risks before they become visible. Prioritizes collaboration health, mobility readiness, and high-visibility user stability.

---

## 13. SaaS Experience Optimization Agent
Analyzes real employee experience on SaaS platforms such as M365, Teams, SAP, Salesforce, or ServiceNow. Correlates endpoint, browser, VPN, Wi-Fi, and network signals to isolate root causes.

---

## 14. Windows 365 & VDI Optimization Agent
Optimizes Citrix, Windows 365, AVD, and Horizon environments by correlating endpoint, virtual session, network, and collaboration telemetry. Reduces latency, reconnects, and hybrid workspace instability.

---

## 15. Green IT & Sustainability Optimization Agent
Identifies Green IT and sustainability opportunities including overnight shutdown, energy optimization, hardware lifecycle extension, responsible digital behaviors, and Engage awareness campaigns.

---

## 16. Collaboration Experience Intelligence Agent
Monitors Teams, Zoom, and Webex collaboration quality across peripherals, drivers, endpoint stability, and network conditions. Helps standardize reliable devices and reduce poor meeting experiences.

---

## 17. Digital Adoption & Feature Usage Intelligence Agent
Measures real adoption of M365, Copilot, Teams, and OneDrive capabilities. Detects underused features, legacy workflows, and opportunities for Adopt, Engage, and contextual enablement.

---

## 18. Shadow IT & AI Usage Intelligence Agent
Detects unmanaged SaaS, AI tools, browser extensions, and unsanctioned workflows. Balances governance and employee productivity by recommending secure alternatives and adoption strategies.

---

## 19. Workplace Cost Optimization Agent
Identifies Digital Workplace cost optimization opportunities including oversized hardware, unused licenses, overprovisioned VDI environments, dormant assets, and excessive support costs — without harming DEX.

---

## 20. Workplace Personas & Segmentation Intelligence Agent
Automatically builds workplace personas from real employee behaviors such as mobility, collaboration habits, VDI usage, support dependency, and digital workstyles. Enables intelligent personalization for all other agents.

---

## 21. Employee Journey Intelligence Agent
Analyzes employee journeys such as onboarding, Windows migration, laptop refresh, Intune onboarding, Copilot rollout, or VDI adoption. Measures friction, stabilization, and transformation success.

---

## 22. Autonomous Remediation Confidence Agent
Measures remediation reliability and determines which Nexthink remediations can safely become autonomous. Enables progressive industrialization of AI-driven self-healing operations.

---

## 23. IT Hygiene & Operational Health Agent
Maintains fleet operational hygiene by detecting unhealthy collectors, excessive uptime, startup degradation, oversized caches, broken services, and accumulated technical debt. Improves long-term endpoint stability.

---

## 24. Change Communication Intelligence Agent
Optimizes Engage campaigns and IT communication by analyzing engagement, timing, communication fatigue, and real behavioral adoption. Enables highly personalized and data-driven change management.

---

## 25. Workforce Sentiment Inference Agent
Identifies populations silently suffering from accumulated digital friction without necessarily opening support tickets. Detects operational fatigue patterns using behavioral and DEX-related telemetry.
